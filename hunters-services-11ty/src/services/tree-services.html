Good to Know

Tree Injections & Treatments FAQs

We treat the pests and diseases that commonly stress Northern California trees — borers, scale, aphids, beetles, and a range of fungal issues — using injections and treatments matched to the species and the problem.
Injections deliver treatment directly into the trunk or root zone, so it reaches the tree from the inside out. This targets the issue efficiently while minimizing impact on beneficial insects and the surrounding landscape.
Look for thinning canopy, early or discolored leaf drop, visible holes or sawdust on the trunk, or sticky residue and sooty mold. If something looks off, a quick assessment will tell you what's going on.
It aligns with our IPM philosophy — targeted, injection-based treatment uses far less product than broad spraying and protects the beneficial insects your landscape depends on.
