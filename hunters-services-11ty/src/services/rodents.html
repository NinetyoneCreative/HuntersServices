Rodent Control & Exclusion in Northern California

Keep rats and mice out for good with smart exclusion — we don't just trap, we seal the entry points that invite them in.

Eco-Friendly IPM Family Owned Since 1992 Free Estimates 4.3★ on Google

Rodents don't just raise the hair on the back of your neck — they chew wiring, contaminate food, and carry health risks into your home or business. And in Northern California, rodent pressure spikes every fall as the weather cools and they look for somewhere warm.

The lasting fix isn't more traps; it's exclusion. Our team removes the active population, then finds and seals the gaps, vents, and gaps along the roofline that let rodents in. Combine that with smart sanitation guidance and you get a home rodents can't get back into.
