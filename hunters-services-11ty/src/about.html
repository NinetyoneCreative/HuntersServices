Who We Are

Three decades of protecting NorCal homes

At Hunters Services, our goal is to deliver California's best value in pest, termite, weed and tree control. Family owned and locally operated by California natives, we pride ourselves on offering environmentally friendly pest and weed solutions.

Established in Oroville in 1992, we were the first eco-friendly pest control company in Northern California, and we've never looked back. Over 30 years later, more than 50,000 homeowners and businesses trust us to keep pests in their place: out of their homes and out of their businesses.

We strive to develop close relationships with the communities we serve. Having been lifelong members of those communities, we want to help create a better future for our cities, one protected home at a time.

Northern California Firsts

We didn't just join the industry, we helped lead it

Across 30+ years, Hunters Services was the first company in the Northern California area to bring homeowners innovations much of the industry later followed:

  • Eco-friendly products, the area's first eco-certified, Integrated Pest Management (IPM) approach, safer for families, pets, and the environment.
  • The power sprayer, first in the region to adopt this more thorough, efficient application method for local homes and businesses.
  • Photos with WDO reports, first to include clear photos in our Wood-Destroying Organisms (termite) reports, so you can see exactly what we found.
  • A dedicated software program, first to run on a purpose-built software system for faster, better-documented, more reliable service.
  • Multiple services under one roof, first to grow beyond just pest control into termites, rodents, wildlife, weed abatement, and tree care.
